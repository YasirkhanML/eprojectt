Animal Kingdom

Animals Are Good Creatures

Animals are kind and amazing creatures. They make our world colorful and lively. Dogs are loyal and protect us. Cats are playful and love to cuddle. Birds sing sweet songs in the trees. Elephants are gentle giants that care for their families. Fish keep our oceans and rivers beautiful. Horses help us travel and work on farms. Every animal has a role in nature. We should love and protect all animals!
