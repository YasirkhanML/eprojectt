Colors EveryWhere

We live in a colorful world! I love all the brightly colored flowers, clothing choices, crayola crayons for coloring sheets, different colored animals, and more! One of the things that young learners need to master is color recognition and color names

Colors can have different effects on children's learning and mood.Learning about colors can be lots of fun! From reading books, making art projects, picking clothes, and looking at the world around them they will learn all the basic colors.
